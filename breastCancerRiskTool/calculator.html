Personalized Results

Although a woman’s risk may be accurately estimated, these predictions do not allow one to say precisely which woman will develop breast cancer. In fact, some woman who do not develop breast cancer have higher risk estimates than some woman who do develop the disease.

As a reminder, this tool was designed for use by health professionals. If you are not a health professional, you are encouraged to print these results and discuss them with your health care provider.

The tool uses a woman’s personal medical and reproductive history and the history of breast cancer among her first-degree relatives(mothers, sisters, daughters) to estimate absolute breast cancer risk-her chance or probability of developing invasive breast cancer in a defined age interval
